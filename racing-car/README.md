### car-race project
#### here, we have a simple car racing video game


### control buttons:
  * [W] --> speed up car
  * [S] --> reduce speed of car
  * [A] -- move left
  * [D] --> move right
   
  you can control my up down left right buttons
  * [UP] --> move car in upward direction (along Y axis)
  * [DOWN] --> move car downwards [ along -Y axis]
